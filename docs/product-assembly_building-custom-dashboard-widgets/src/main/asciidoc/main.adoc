// Purpose statement for the assembly
As a business analyst or business rules developer, you will use the page builder to design and define your page and determine what all information can be displayed through it. You can decide what all core, navigational and reporting components are a part of your page. You can also customize the properties of the components of the page as per your requirements. It is recommended that you define a data provider prior to creating a page.

.Prerequisite
You must have sufficient permissions for creating the pages.
