// Purpose statement for the assembly
As a business processes developer, you can use {CENTRAL} in {PRODUCT} or the {PRODUCT} BPMN modeler in VSCode to design business processes to meet specific business requirements. This document describes business processes and the concepts and options for creating them using the process designer in {PRODUCT}. This document also describes the BPMN2 elements in {PRODUCT}. For more details about BPMN2, see the https://www.omg.org/spec/BPMN/2.0/About-BPMN[Business Process Model and Notation Version 2.0] specification.

.Prerequisites
* {EAP_LONG} {EAP_VERSION} is installed. For details, see https://access.redhat.com/documentation/en-us/red_hat_jboss_enterprise_application_platform/{EAP_VERSION}/html-single/installation_guide/[_{EAP_LONG} {EAP_VERSION} Installation Guide_].
* {PRODUCT} is installed and configured with {KIE_SERVER}. For more information, see {URL_INSTALLING_AND_CONFIGURING}#assembly-install-on-eap[_{INSTALLING_ON_EAP}_].
//xreflink
* {PRODUCT} is running and you can log in to {CENTRAL} with the `developer` role. For more information, see {URL_INSTALLING_AND_CONFIGURING}#assembly-planning[_{PLANNING_INSTALL}_].
