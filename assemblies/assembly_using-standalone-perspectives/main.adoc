// Purpose statement for the assembly
As a business rules developer, you can embed standalone perspectives from {CENTRAL} in your web application and then use them to edit rules, processes, decision tables, and other assets.

.Prerequisites
* Ensure {CENTRAL} is deployed and is running on a web/application server.
* Ensure you are logged in to {CENTRAL}.
